Lasagna Recipe

Description

Everyone loves a good lasagna, right? It's a great way to feed a crowd and a perfect dish to bring to a potluck. It freezes well. It reheats well. Leftovers will keep you happy for days.

This classic lasagna is made with an easy meat sauce as the base. Layer the sauce with noodles and cheese, then bake until bubbly! This is great for feeding a big family, and freezes well, too.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef chuck
  • 1/2 medium onion, diced (about 3/4 cup)
  • 1 (28-ounce)can good-quality tomato sauce
  • 3 ounces tomato paste (half a 6-ounce can)
  • 1 (14 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 15 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 pound dry lasagna noodles (requires 9 lasagna noodles - unbroken)

Steps

  1. Start by making the sauce with ground beef, onions and a combo of tomato sauce, tomato paste, and crushed tomatoes.
  2. Let this simmer while you boil the noodles and get the ricotta ready.
  3. From there, it's just an assemble a layer of noodles, sauce, followed by a layer of cheese. Repeat until you have three layers and have used up all the ingredients.
  4. Bake until bubbly and you're ready to eat!
