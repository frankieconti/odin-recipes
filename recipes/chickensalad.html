Chicken Salad Recipe

Description

My mother and father make the best chicken salad. It's a joint effort; my father assembles the ingredients, while my mom makes the dressing.

This family favorite chicken salad is made with celery, bell pepper, green olives, apple, lettuce, and mayoâ€”plus a secret ingredient that makes all the difference!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound raw boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into 2 1/2-inch chunks (or 2 to 3 cups cooked chicken meat)
  • 2 ribs celery, chopped
  • 1/2 red bell pepper, seeded and chopped
  • 4 to 6 green olives, pitted and minced
  • 1/4 cup chopped red onion
  • 1/2 to 1 apple, cored and chopped
  • 1/3 head iceberg lettuce, sliced and chopped

Steps

  1. Poach the chicken until cooked. After let the chicken sit for 15 minutes.
  2. Mix the chopped celery, bell pepper, olives, red onion, and apple
  3. Remove the chicken from the poaching water and dice it. (Or dice already cooked chicken if that is what you are using for this salad.) Mix it in with the and vegetables.
  4. Add lettuce and Eat!
