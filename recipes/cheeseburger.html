Cheeseburger Recipe

Description

In the summertime, I like nothing more than grilling some burgers outside with friends and family, but you shouldnâ€™t have to wait for warmer weather to get your burger fix in.

Want a great burger cooked on the stovetop? These double-stacked cheeseburgers are what your cravings desire: thin, tender patties, melty cheese, and a generous amount of secret sauce. Don't forget a napkin!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons ketchup
  • 20 to 24 ounces ground beef (90/10)
  • 4 to 8 slices American cheese
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • Fresh lettuce
  • Tomato slices

Steps

  1. Make the burger sauce. Stir together the mayonnaise and ketchup
  2. Make the burger patties. Measure out ground beef into eight 3-ounce patties. Add to pan.
  3. Assemble the burgers onto hamburger buns. Add cheese slices, fresh lettuce and tomato slices.
  4. Eat!
